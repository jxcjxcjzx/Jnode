 title : The OINKweb Open Source Project on Open Hub
 summary : <p>At this point, OINK is put on ice, but I might revive it at some point.
<br/>OINKweb, or just OINK, as I like to call it is supposed to be a fully modular and highly configurable content management system written in php and built to support more or less any database that people care to use.</p>

<p>People might ask, why we need another CM System, and the answer might very well be that we don&#39;t. This probably is the case. There are lots of other options out there, and this will probably not be better than many of those. However I want to use it and it may just be the right for you (when I release it someday), and that&#39;s all the reason I need.</p>
